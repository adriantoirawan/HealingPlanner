About Us

The Healing Planner is a lightweight, local-first web application designed to help users curate
and select relaxation activities without decision fatigue.

Problem Statement
Target Audience

When burned out, the cognitive load required to plan a relaxing activity often leads to "doomscrolling" or doing nothing at all.

Users lack a centralized, low-friction place to store their relaxation ideas and need a way to bypass decision fatigue when it's time to actually take a break

We design this platform for professionals and students who are dealing with stress and burnout, helping them find better ways to relax and recharge.

also for indecisive individuals who struggle with the "what should we do today?" question.

Tech Stack

"We deliver a fast, responsive tool that categorizes healing ideas and intelligently
recommends activities based on the user's budget".
